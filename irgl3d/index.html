About us

INFORMATICS
RALLY GAMES
& LOGIC

IRGL
(Informatics Rally Games and Logic)

is an annual competition for high school students held by students of the Petra Christian University Informatics Study Program. Carrying the concept of rally games, participants will solve logic and math and play a variety of games that are presented to achieve the title of champion.

This year, IRGL carries the theme "The Lost Kingdom: Odyssey of the Sacred Excalibur." Tells the story of a legendary sword belonging to a long lost kingdom. The person who can find and successfully draw the sword will get the power to restore the lost kingdom. Many people try searched for the sword, but nothing worked because of the sword hidden in the wilderness that holds many challenges and mysteries.
